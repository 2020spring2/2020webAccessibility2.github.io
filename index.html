Text

Page Title

The title of your page is what is the shown in the title bar in a few browsers and shown on the tab. They are also what appear in the search engine results. One of the most important things to do with your page's title is to place the most important information at the beginning of the title as some titles become too long for the tab to show. You should always test your title in multiple browsers to make sure it can accurately be read by more than just your default.

'Alt' Text

The 'alt' text of the image(or illustration, or chart, and etc.) that is shown is designed to describe the image if it does not load on the page or if someone is using a screen reader. This text should inform the reader of what the content you have chosen is trying to explain in a format that is easily understandable.
